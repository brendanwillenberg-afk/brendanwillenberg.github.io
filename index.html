Who am I?

Hi. I'm Brendan and I think I'm a slight unicorn in the fact that I am a big brand/platform creative leader with brilliant digital and CX skills. Yes, I used the term Unicorn.

I've been working as a creative since 2006, a creative director since 2015 and an Executive Creative Director since 2020.

After watching too much Grand Designs, I left a cushy ECD role at Clemenger and completed a full knockdown rebuild of my skills, working at a variety of specialist agencies who taught me… in the words of Kevin McCloud, shed loads.

You'll currently find me working as ECD at Innocean Australia.

I like to combine Media and a bit of Magic to create famous ideas that take, break or remix culture. In my career I've won over 110 awards both locally and internationally. I've also been featured in the AdNews Top 20 Creative Power list a few times.

In June 2022 I was named 5th best ECD in APAC by One Show. I led Clemenger BBDO Sydney to AWARD Agency of the Year in 2019. I was a founding partner of TKT — AdNews Small Agency of the Year 2018. And I won a Gold APAC Effie for long term effectiveness on V Energy Drink.

From 2015–2018 I ran AWARD school. Being asked to run the school was a massive honour as I got my start after winning AWARD School in 2006.

That's enough sell… what do you do outside of work?

When I'm not working I spend way too much time collecting records. Check out my insta if you dig soul, jazz and other oddities or, tune into Eastside FM on Tuesday at 6:30 to hear my radio show EXPANSIONS. You'll also find me in various clubs around Sydney.
